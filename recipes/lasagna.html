Lasagna

Description

It is made using noodles, meat sause, noodles, cheese, mozzarella slices, and repeat the layeers then top with the remaining Parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Gorgonzola cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Came

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion and cook, stirring frequently, until softened and golden brown.
  3. Add carrots and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender.
  4. Add ground beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until browned and no longer pink.
  5. Add Parmesan, mozzarella, Romano, Gorgonzola, and Feta cheeses and cook, stirring until melted and bubbly.
  6. Add lasagna noodles and cook, stirring occasionally, until al dente.
  7. Serve the lasagna on a baking sheet and top with remaining cheese.
